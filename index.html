ABOUT THE FEST

What is TechFusion 2.0?

TechFusion 2.0 is a celebration of innovation, creativity, and technological excellence, bringing together passionate minds to build impactful solutions for real-world challenges.

Hosted by Oculus Aegis, the innovation and technology club of the CSE - IoT, Cybersecurity including Blockchain department at DSATM, this 3-day tech fest brings together innovators, developers, and designers to explore and solve real-world challenges at the forefront of emerging technologies.

Featuring an 6-hour Ideathon, 3-hour E - Sports and an intense 24-hour Hackathon, TechFusion 2.0 provides a platform for participants to transform ideas into impactful technological solutions.

Whether you're a coder, designer, or visionary problem-solver — TechFusion 2.0 is the stage to innovate, collaborate, and build the future.

FAQ

Got Questions?

Who can participate? +

Anyone with a passion for technology and innovation can participate. Students from any college, branch, or year are welcome to join, either individually or as a team, depending on the event.

Is there a registration fee? +

Yes, a nominal registration fee is applicable for participation. The fee may vary depending on the event.

Can I participate in both events? +

Yes, participants can register for multiple events. However, you cannot participate in both the Ideathon and E-Sports tournament as they are scheduled on the same day and may run in parallel after a certain time.

What should I bring for the Hackathon? +

Participants are required to bring their own laptops, chargers, and any necessary hardware or accessories. Ensure your system has all required software and development tools pre-installed. A stable internet connection, extension boards (if needed), and personal essentials are also recommended for a smooth experience.

Will there be mentors available? +

Absolutely. Industry mentors and faculty guides will be available throughout both events to help you refine ideas and solutions.

How are projects judged? +

Projects are evaluated on innovation, feasibility, impact, execution quality, and presentation by a panel of industry experts and faculty.

Will participants receive certificates? +

Yes! All registered participants receive participation certificates. Winners receive special merit certificates along with prizes.

WHO WE ARE

About Oculus Aegis

Oculus Aegis is the official Design & Technology Club of the Department of CSE in IoT and Cybersecurity including Blockchain at Dayananda Sagar Academy of Technology and Management (DSATM), Bengaluru.

We are a community driven by design thinking, creative problem-solving, and cutting-edge technology, parallely organizing workshops, hackathons, design sprints, and events that bridge the gap between academic learning and industry practice.

Our vision: To nurture the next generation of tech innovators who build with empathy, design with purpose, and code with passion.
